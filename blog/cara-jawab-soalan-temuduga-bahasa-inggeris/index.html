Cara Jawab Soalan Temuduga Dalam Bahasa Inggeris

Tak tahu nak jawab apa bila soalan temuduga dalam bahasa Inggeris?

Dalam artikel ini kita akan berbincang tentang cara menjawab soalan temuduga dalam bahasa Inggeris. Semoga ia bermanfaat buat kita semua.

Contoh jawapan yang kami berikan ini, anda boleh ubah dan sesuaikan mengikut jawatan yang anda pohon.

Contoh Soalan Temuduga Dalam Bahasa Inggeris Dan Contoh Jawapan

Cara menjawab soalan temuduga dalam bahasa Inggeris.

# Why Do You Want To Work In This Company?

Cara menjawab yang salah:

Because I like to work here. I like this job. I love this industry.

Apa yang anda perlu jawab:

I’ve always loved administration (contoh bidang) because I am an organized person.

I like things to be organized and systematic. I work well under stress so I can handle any kind of task with minimal supervision.

# Tell Us About Yourself / Introduce Yourself

Cara menjawab yang salah:

My name is Siti. I was born in 15 October 1990 in Selangor.

My mother is a housewife and my father is a soldier. I graduated from UTHM in 2015. I studied Business Administration.

I have never worked before.

Apa yang anda perlu jawab:

My name is Siti and I’m 25 years old. I have a degree in Business Administration.

Although I haven’t had any working experience, I’ve spent my free time reading business articles and researching about latest business news just to keep me updated.

I’m hoping to be given a chance to experience the real world of business

# Why Did You Quit Your Last Job?

Cara menjawab yang salah:

I quit because my last job was terrible. The workload was so heavy and the boss was so pushy. My previous boss was nightmare.

Peringatan:
Jangan kutuk/burukkan bekas majikan/syarikat yang anda pernah bekerja. Ia memberikan impresi buruk kepada anda.

Apa yang anda perlu jawab:

I loved my last job. It taught me so many things and my previous boss has always been helpful.

But I quit because I want to experience new environment and broaden my horizon by working in a different company.

# Where Do You See Yourself In Five Years?

Cara menjawab yang salah:

Relaxing in Thailand

atau

Being a boss in this company

Apa yang anda perlu jawab:

I hope to become very well-experienced in what I do and have a better understanding of my field.

# What Is Your Greatest Weakness?

Cara menjawab yang salah:

I tend to put things to the very last minute.

Peringatan:
Kelemahan anda harus disokong dengan kekuatan anda.

Apa yang anda perlu jawab:

I tend to put things to the very last minute.

But I work well under pressure and I personally think that pressure motivates me to work twice as hard and finish my work on time.

# What Salary Are You Looking For?

Cara menjawab yang salah:

I hope to get a suitable salary that would be able to support me, living in this economy and in this big city.

Apa yang anda perlu jawab:

I hope to get a salary that is suitable with my qualification and job duties. I also want to be considered based on my working experience.

# Why Should I Hire You?

Cara menjawab yang salah:

Because I’m the best candidate.

atau

Because you’re hiring.

Apa yang anda perlu jawab:

I’m a team player, I work well under pressure, and I’m a people person.

I can work with all kinds of people and am always willing to learn new things and tackle new challenges.

Penutup
